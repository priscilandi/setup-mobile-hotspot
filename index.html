How to Setup Mobile Hotspot

Objective

To share your phone's cellular data with other devices, or connect to the internet without relying on public Wi-Fi networks.

Prerequisites

  • A compatible device (smartphone, tablet, or dedicated hotspot device) with the capability to create a hotspot.
  1. On your computer, click the Start button (bottom-left corner of the screen).
  2. Type Mobile Hotspot.
  3. Click the option that says Mobile Hotspot on settings.
  1. A window will open with the Mobile Hotspot page.>
  2. Under Share my Internet Connection From:

    3.        -Choose Wi-Fi (if you are connected to Wi-Fi).

           -Choose Ethernet (if you are connected to ethernet).

If you are not connected to the internet, you must connect first, your hotspot won't work otherwise.

  1. Under the section that says Properties,you will see:

    2.     -Network name: This is the Wi-Fi name others will see (you can change it).

        -Network password: This is the password other people need to connect

  2. To change them:
  3. Type your preferred network name (example: MyLaptopHotspot).
  4. Type a new password (at least 8 characters).
  5. Click Save.
  1. At the top of the window, you will see a switch labeled Mobile Hotspot.
  2. Click the switch to turn in ON.
  3. You will now see a message like: "Your mobile hotspot is active. Devices can connect using Wi-Fi".

    Now that your computer is broadcasting Wi-Fi, connect to another device:

  1. On your phone or tablet, open "Wi-Fi Settings".
  2. Find the name of your computerâ€™s hotspot (the name you saw or created earlier).
  3. Tap it and enter the password.
  4. You should now be connected!
  1. Back on your computer:

    2.      -"X device(s) connecte: - this means a device has joined.

    If you don't see anything, double-check the password and that the device is in range.

Process Complete

The device has succesfully joined Mobile Hotspot!
